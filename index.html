Publications

  • Commutation Relations in Yangian-type Algebras  in progress

    I prove several properties of certain commutators in \(U(\mathfrak{gl}_N(\mathcal{A}))\) that were discovered using my program

  • PSWF-Radon approach to reconstruction from band-limited Hankel transform  in progress, to be submitted to Inverse Problems

    We give new formulas for inversion of band-limited Hankel transform of integer and half-integer order, based on prolate spheroidal wave functions.

  • Insolvability of xx = a in elementary functions

    We prove that the equation \(x^x = a\) is not solvable in elementary functions.

  • Solvability of Equations in Elementary Functions  Journal of Knot Theory & Its Ramifications, 29(2)

    We prove that the equation \(\tan(x) - x = a\) is not solvable in elementary functions.
